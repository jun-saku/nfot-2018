Sining Tanghalan

Showcase of Talents and Skills in Arts, Music, and Performance

About

Sining Tanghalan is the showcase of talents and culture in music, arts, dance, creative writing, and performance.The different competitions in each category are found below.

Bayle sa Kalye

Bayle sa Kalye is a modern/contemporary street dance skills exhibition anchored on a specific theme.

Likhawitan

Likhawitan is an OPM songwriting competition and performance.

Pintahusay

Pintahusay is an on-the-spot poster making competition.

Sineliksik

Sineliksik is a shortfilm competition which includes the shooting and editing of the film.

Sulatanghal

Sulatanghal is a playwriting competitions in which the skills in creative writing is tested.

Manik-Aninong Dulaan

Manik-Aninong Dulaan is a scriptwriting and shadow puppetry competitions in which skills in music, theatre, visual arts, and creative writing is tested.
