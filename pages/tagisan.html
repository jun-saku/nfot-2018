Tagisan ng Talento

Showcase of Talents and Skills in Filipino

About

Tagisan ng Talento is the showcase of talents which includes verbal and language skills in the Filipino language. The different competitions in each category are found below.

Dagliang Talumpati

Dagliang Talumpati is an impromptu speech delivery competition in which the critical thinking and verbal skills are tested.

Interpretatibong Pagbasa

Interpretatibong Pagbasa is the proper and correct delivery of a speech, monologue, declamation, and poem.
