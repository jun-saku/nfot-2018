Special Program in the Foreign Language

Showcase of Talents and Skills in Spanish, French, German, Japanese, and Mandarin Chinese.

About

The Foreign Language Skills Exhibition displays the talents and skills of students in the different foreign language. The different competitions in each category are found below.

Characters on Parade

Characters on Parade is a cosplay competition of a famous character in the different foreign languages.

Singing Idol

Singing Idol is the showcase of talents in singing songs in a foreign language.

Quiz Whiz

Quiz Whiz challenges the knowledge of students in the politics, literature, language and culture of the different foreign languages.
