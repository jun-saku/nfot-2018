Population Development

Showcase of Knowledge and Skills in Social Sciences

About

Population Development is the showcase of knowledge and skills in social sciences and other issues in our society. The different competitions in each category are found below.

PopDev Debate

PopDev Debate is a combination of argumentation and debate that is conducted in a modified round table discussion.

Pop Quiz

Pop Quiz is a quiz based on concepts in Population Education Core Messages and Key Concepts.

On-the-spot Poster Making

On-the-spot Poster Making is the construction of a poster based on a given theme about social sciences.

Essay Writing Contest

Essay Writing Contest is all about the making of an original essay composition about a certain theme.

Jingle Writing and Singing Contest

Jingle Writing and Singing Contest is the composition of an original jingle which is performed in acapella.
