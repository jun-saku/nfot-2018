About

The National Festival of Talents is a yearly event in which students from all around the country showcase their talents, knowledge, and skills in the different competitions in each category

Peace

The National Festival of Talents is not only about competitions. It is also about the culture, sportsmanship, and peace during the event. The goal of this event is to recognize that Filipinos, even at competitions, are peaceful and will work together.

Unity

Working together to achieve a common goal is what unity is all about. As Filipinos, it is in our culture to help each other in times of need and in the competitions, we work together to find the best among the best.

Progress

In performing each competition, we improve as time passes and these innovations can help improve our country. We can utilize these innovations and ideas to progress and develop our economy for the betterment of the people.
